Coping Strategies 🌟

These are tools you can use when things feel overwhelming. Try different ones and see what works for you!

🌬️

Deep Breathing

Take slow, deep breaths. Breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 4, breathe out for 4.

✍️

Journaling

Write down your thoughts and feelings. It helps to get them out of your head.

🎨

Creative Expression

Draw, paint, or create something. Art can help express what words can't.

🏃

Physical Activity

Move your body! Dance, walk, stretch - movement helps release stress.

🧘

Mindfulness

Focus on the present moment. Notice 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear.

💬

Talk It Out

Share your feelings with someone you trust. You don't have to go through this alone.

🆘 Crisis Safety Plan

Step 1: Recognize Warning Signs

You might be experiencing:

  • Overwhelming feelings of sadness or hopelessness
  • Thoughts of harming yourself
  • Feeling completely alone
  • Intense anxiety or panic

Step 2: GET HELP NOW

📞 Call/Text 1737

Youthline

Step 3: Immediate Coping Strategies

  • 🧊 Hold ice cubes in your hands
  • 🌬️ Take 10 deep breaths
  • 🚶 Go to a safe, public place
  • 📞 Call a trusted adult
  • 🐾 Pet an animal if available
